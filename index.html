After the heuristic evaluation, the team and I could make the problem statement and empathy map

Problem Statement

We believe that redesigning the main navigation and updating the UI will help animal lovers find how they can help quick and easy to provide more for the Organization. How Might we make the navigation more Intuitive for Animal Lovers who are looking to help by adopting, donating, or volunteering?

Now It's Time To Ideate

After the Ideation, I could Prototype, Once we hit the prototyping section, I started really getting excited. This is probably my favorite part about UI. I was in charge of organizing the mobile mid-fidelity prototype, and was able to get it looking really sweet for the testing. I also noticed that the current logo was horrendous, and I went and designed a new one which the users really ended up liking.
